Description

Nanakramguda temple or Sri Ranganadha swamy temple is a 400-year-old Ranganatha temple in Nanakramguda, near Manikonda in Hyderabad.[1] It is located at Rangbagh in Nanakramguda, which is approximately 15 km from Mehdipatnam. • Temple is over 400 years old, origin of which is unknown. • Temple is being maintained by a Rajasthani family, Pitti Deva. • Sri Ranganathaswamy idol is small, well carved, made out of a single stone. • Apart from Sri devi and Bhoodevi, idol of Neela devi is also seen in the sanctum. • The utsava moorthy, idol of Godha Devi (Aandal) made out of copper was found in the temple tank over 100 years back.
