NANDEESWARA SANEESWARA SWAMY TEMPLE

  1. Home
  2. NANDEESWARA SANEESWARA SWAMY TEMPLE

Description

Nandeeswara Saneeswara Swamy Temple is in Nandi Vaddeman, Nagar Kurnool Mandal in Mehabubnagar District, Telangana. Nandi Vaddeman village is also known as Temples Village. Shani Bhagavan is the son for Sun god and Chaya Devi. Sani Bagawan likes black color and Saturday is his favorite day and he is the brother of Yama Dharmaraja. He is also know as daivakary and his Vahanam is crow. In Shani Shingnapur Temple women’s are not allowed to perform any poojas but at Nandi Vaddeman Shani Bhagavan temple women’s are allowed to perform all pooja’s to the main deity. Puranas says that this temple was built in 12th century and this temple was built by Gona Ganna Reddy and Gona Budda Reddy. Sri Saneeswara Swamy Temple is the famous Shani temple with the idol measuring 7½ feet. The idol is placed along with Jyeshta Devi on his vahanam which makes it even more attractive. The festival Shani Trayodashi is celebrated here with great devotion by the devotees. Special pujas are conducted for the mankind. The temple is 5 kms away from Bijinapally Mandal head quarters and 40 kilometers away from Mahabubnagar.
